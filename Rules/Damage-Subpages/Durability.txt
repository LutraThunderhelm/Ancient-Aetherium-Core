Durability is a measure of any given items physical state, but is most commonly seen in Husk limbs and various tools that may be operated by any given character. Any physical damage causes something to lose Durability, whether that be from combat or from the enviroment, Durability is lost whenever any given damage exceeds an items toughness. Losing Durability tends to hinder an items performance while still allowing it to function, while losing all Durability does not mean an item is fully destroyed, but that item can no longer be used.
