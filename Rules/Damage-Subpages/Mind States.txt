Husks, body parts, tools, those all come and go. Most parts of a Husk can be easily replaced in the right conditions, except 1. The Mind Crystal is where a Bear's entire being is stored, and at its core it is the Bear's collection of memories, thoughts, and actions, which pilots a dead husk of a body. This Mind Crystal is physically disconnected from the Husk in a way that allows to transfer between Husks, as well as lose entire parts of their bodies without issue, but upon taking damage the Mind Crystal itself changes a Bear entirely.
