The Clothing layer of Attire is typically host to ranges of repurposed human clothes now remade to fit the bears who need them. Clothing Attire itself is often built for the purpose of carrying as much as possible, reversing their listed Volume Scores. Becoming the defacto carrying capacity of any worthy adventurer, Clothing Attire's Volume Score is a value of how much capacity the Clothing Attire has to carry other items, such as a bag with 2 Pots of space, allowing it to hold up to 2 Pots of other items. Additionally, Clothing Attire tends to be weak, falling apart at the slightest bit of damage, giving Clothing Attire generally low Toughness. Moving items in and out of Clothing Attire requires 1 Half Action.
