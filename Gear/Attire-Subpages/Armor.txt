Armor Attire is used to deny the change for damage to make it to ones Husk. Being the top layer of Attire and generally made from protective materials, Armor Attire is designed for the specific purpose of defense in combat, or survivablity in the Ruins. Armor Attire adds its Toughness Value to the respective part of the Husk it is worn on, such as the Torso when worn as a Top. Armor will tend to have higher Toughness than its peers, and will have 3 Durability when targeted specifically, instead of a Limb.
