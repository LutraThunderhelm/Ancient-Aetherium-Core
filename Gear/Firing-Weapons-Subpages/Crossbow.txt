Crossbow Firing Weapons are traditional and widely used Firing Weapons with the ability to use specialized bolts to provide unique effects to each shot. Crossbow Firing Weapons tend to only be able to load a single shot at once, and use Bolts, a type of ammunition that can be easily found and made, as well as easily modified. The ability to readily find Crossbow Firing Weapon ammunition makes maintaining a Crossbow cheap, allowing them to become widespread as an efficient weapon of destruction. Crossbows take one Half Action to reload.
