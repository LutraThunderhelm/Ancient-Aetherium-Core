Blades refers to weapons designed with the explicit purpose of destroying en enemy. Blades tend to be smaller one handed swords and axes designed to cut through the thinner metals used by Hive Droids. Blades are simple and straight forward weapons, only built to deliver pain and destruction. Blades have no inherient special effects for being a Blade, instead just being normal weapons with higher and more consistant damaging abilities. All blades add the users Brawling Level to their damage, unless stated otherwise.
